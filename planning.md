## **HTML tags**
**&lt;Head&gt;:**
Surround header material such as titles, CSS info, and company slogans.

**&lt;IMG&gt;:**
Used to display an image.

**&lt;P&gt;:**
  Used for paragraphs.

**&lt;H1&gt;,&lt;H2&gt;,...:**
  A heading line that is used to mark the sections of a document.

## **CSS tags**
