Moka

Moka is a fluffy dragon alien with green "G"s on their cheek. They can also have visible scars on their chest. They are not from the Antihuman universe but ended up there after their spaceship malfunctioned and crashed in Golden City. Now they are looking for Ki and away back to their home universe, while investigating ANTIHUMAN INC.

Morale

Morale is the CEO of a makeup company located in the Central Tower. She was infected with The Roots during the events of Lab Rat, leading to her becoming the second test subject in attempt to save her life. Her alien form is that of a flower. Morale possesses no mouth but still has a human like skull. she is part of the Gossip Girls as seen at the end of Lab Rat.

First Appearance: Lab Rat

Vanilla Milkshake

Vanilla Milkshake (VM) was infected with The Roots virus before his Metamorphosis. He became the first subject of Green Ichor. His alien design is a bat-like creature. VM got all of the staff at ANHIHUMAN INC. infected with The Roots. His mental state is very unstable. VM was considered a failed test do to how bad his infection was. The Lavender flower has shown up in both music videos that VM has appeared in. WHITE?

First Appearance: Data_Reject
