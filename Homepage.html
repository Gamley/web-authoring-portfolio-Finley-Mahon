Morale

Morale is the CEO of a makeup company located in the Central Tower. She was infected with The Roots during the events of Lab Rat, leading to her becoming the second test subject in attempt to save her life. Her alien form is that of a flower. Morale possesses no mouth but still has a human like skull. She is part of the Gossip Girls as seen at the end of Lab Rat.

First Appearance: Lab Rat

Vanilla Milkshake

Vanilla Milkshake (VM) was infected with The Roots virus before his Metamorphosis. He became the first subject of Green Ichor. His alien design is a bat-like creature. VM got all of the staff at ANHIHUMAN INC. infected with The Roots. His mental state is very unstable. VM was considered a failed test do to how bad his infection was. The Lavender flower has shown up in both music videos that VM has appeared in. WHITE?

First Appearance: Data_Reject

Green Ichor

Green Ichor was made by ANTIHUMAN Inc. as a experimental vaccine for The Roots. Anyone injected with the vaccine will go through a process called "Metamorphosis" turning them into an alien. The word "Ichor" means "the fluid that flows like blood in the veins of the gods."

First Appearance: Lab Rat

Roots (virus)

Hcov-oc43E also known as "The Roots" is an airborne pathogen that is part of the coronavirus family. The Roots only infects humans showing up as black veins on the arms, chest, and neck before eventually attacking the heart and brain.

First Appearance: Lab Rat

Central Tower

The Central Tower (CT) as the name implies is in the centre of Golden City, housing several different companies including ANTIHUMAN INC. The ring around CT is home to the Council. CT is alive and has drones as seen in Lab Rat. CT has colour recognition as it can recognize the blue of the CT uniform.

First Appearance: Y2K
